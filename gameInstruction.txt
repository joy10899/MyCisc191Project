The game will have 2 players.

Please enter the names of 2 players to get started.

You can choose Animal Game or Number Game according to 
your preference to the type of cards you like.

Each game display multiple cards facedown.

When you click on a card, it would play a sound or 
an animation according to the type of the game.

If you find out 2 cards match, your score go up.

Otherwise, you'll lose your turn and the game will switch player.

Whoever get higher score will be the winner.
